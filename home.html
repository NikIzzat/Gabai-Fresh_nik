About Products

Welcome to Gabai Fresh, your trusted source for authentic Moringa tea. Unlike many others in Malaysia who import their Moringa tea from places like Sri Lanka—leaving questions about its authenticity—we take pride in offering a truly original product. At Gabai Fresh, everything begins in our own garden in Sungai Gabai, Selangor. We personally cultivate Moringa saplings without the use of chemical fertilizers, ensuring that every step of the process remains natural and pure.


Each tea leaf is carefully handpicked and processed manually, preserving its quality and health benefits. We believe that using original ingredients and traditional methods results in a tea that is not only more effective but also more beneficial for your body. Mori-Ori is more than just a tea; it’s a labor of love and dedication to bring you the very best nature has to offer. Thank you for choosing Gabai Fresh!
